Boook Report:Wonder by R.J Palacio

Introduction

Wonder tells a story of August Pullman,a boy born with a rare facial difference. After being homeschooled for years, Auggie enters middle school for the first time, facing challenges that test his courage.

Book Cover

Author's Background

R.J Palacio is an American autho and graphic designer. She wrote Wonderin 2012, inspired by a real-life encounter with a child who had a craniofacial condition. The book has since become a bestseller and has been adapted into a film.

Plot Summary

The story follow Auggie as he navigate middle schoo, making friends while dealing with bullying and people's reactions to his appearance.
