Book Report:Wonder by R.J Palacio

Introduction

Wonder tells a story of August, "Auggie" Pullman, who is a boy with a rare facial difference. After being homeschooled for years, Auggie enters middle school for the first time, facing challenges that test his courages.

Key Themes

  • Kindness:Small acts of kindness makes a big diffence.
  • Empathy: Understanding others' feelings helps build connections.
  • Courage: It takes courage to be yourself, especially when others judge you.
